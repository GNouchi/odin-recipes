    Pot Roast (2)

  • 2 lb chuck
  • 2 springs Rosemary
  • Chicken stock or water to cover
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Flour

    Directions:

  1. Generously Salt and pepper meat.
  2. Coat in Flour.
  3. Hard sear on medium high heat to brown meat.
  4. Simmer with herbs in stock or water

    5. [1 hr + 1/2 hr for each 1 lb of meat.]
